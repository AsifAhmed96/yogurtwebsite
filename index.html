Message from our founder

Meat yogurt, known as "yogurtlu kebap" in Turkish cuisine, is a savory dish featuring tender cubes of lamb or beef cooked in a rich, flavorful sauce, then combined with creamy yogurt. This Middle Eastern and Mediterranean delight offers a harmonious blend of succulent meat and the smooth, tangy notes of yogurt, creating a satisfying and comforting meal. It is often served with aromatic spices and garnished with fresh herbs, making it a delicious and wholesome culinary experience.

Chicken Yogurt

"Chicken yogurt" typically refers to a dish featuring succulent chicken pieces marinated in a creamy yogurt-based mixture infused with spices and herbs. The yogurt not only adds a delightful tanginess but also helps tenderize the chicken, resulting in a flavorful and tender chicken dish.

Meat Yogurt

"Meat Yogurt" features tender cubes of lamb or beef cooked to perfection and then combined with a rich, creamy yogurt-based sauce. The yogurt imparts a smooth and tangy contrast to the savory meat, creating a harmonious and satisfying flavor profile.

Fish Yogurt

"Fish Yogurt" involves gently poached or baked fish fillets, which are then served with a yogurt-based sauce infused with garlic, lemon juice, and fresh herbs like dill or mint. The yogurt adds a refreshing and tangy element to the dish, complementing the natural sweetness of the fish.
